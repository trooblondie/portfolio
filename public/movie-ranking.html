Movies Worth Your Time

Top 3 Movies of 2023

Oppenhiemer

It's no surprise that this movie has swept awards season. Christopher Nolan and Cillian Murphy smashed it out of the park with this blockbuster hit.

Barbie

Hater's gonna hate but this movie was so well done! Acting was superb. Writing was fantastic. And it was just the right amount of topical to make you question harmful societal constructs.

Spider-Man Across the Spider-Verse

The animation style of this series is killer! And the storyline adds life back to a beat to death franchise.
